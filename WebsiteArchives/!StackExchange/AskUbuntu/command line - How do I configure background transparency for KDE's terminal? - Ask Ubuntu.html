I guess you mean Konsole by the KDE Terminal. Here's how to set background transparency:

  1. Click Menu Settings
  2. Click Configure Current Profile... / Edit Current Profile...
  3. Choose tab Appearance
  4. Click Edit...
  5. Choose the background transparency you wish.
  6. Enjoy.
